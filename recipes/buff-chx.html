Buffalo Chicken Flautas (a.k.a. Taquitos)

Creamy, cheesy buffalo chicken goodness in a flash

These buffalo chicken taquitos/flautas are just as delicious as they are easy to make. This recipe will make 10-12, depending on how many tortillas come in the pack you buy. You can adjust the buffalo sauce for spice/flavor and adjust the cheese to chicken ratio depending on your needs for the day. Some days my diet lacks fats so I have to make it a point to add some in a somewhat healthy way. This is one example of how I accomplish that. you could always get bigger tortillas and add in some rice and beans and top it with some veggies if you are wanting a quick but satisfying flavor excursion. Easy to make a big batch to throw in the freezer for later. Possibilities are endless with this one and the flavor is phenomenal! Enjoy!

  • Cream Cheese (1/2lb to 1 lb)
  • Ricotta Cheese (1/2 to a full tub)
  • Bleu Cheese (1/4 to 1/2 wedge)
  • Buffalo Sauce (to taste)
  • Shredded Chicken (about 1-2lb)
  • Tortillas (10-12 Your Choice)

  1. Slow cook the chicken until tender (2-4 hours depending on cooker & settings)
  2. Shred chicken (falls apart on its own if you used a slow cooker)
  3. Preheat oven to 380F
  4. Mix up all the cheeses and sauce (Magic Bullet works great!)
  5. Combine chicken and cheese/sauce concoction
  6. Spoon mixture into tortillas (it takes less than you think...about 2 Tbsp per tortilla)
  7. Roll those suckers up nice and tight like you're back in college
  8. Bake until golden and crispy (spraying them will a little oil is an option here)
  9. Do the damn dishes while the things cook, you bum!
  10. Throw in freezer when completely cool
  11. You are now 65 seconds (in microwave) away from delicious protien snacks at any given moment
