Sweet Potato Fries

Not too salty. Not too sweet. Delicious. Nutritious. Easy as hell to make.

This recipe is literally possible to screw up. Everything is to taste and nothing about it is a science. From the amounts to the spices to the oven temp and timing. You literally CANNOT go wrong with this classic. Amazing taste and ridiculously simple process! If you're not careful, people might think you actually know what you are doing in the kitchen.

  • Sweet Potatoes (to hunger)
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil (enough to coat potatoes)
  • Salt and Pepper (to taste)

  1. Preheat oven to about 400F
  2. Chop potatoes evenly into desired shape
  3. Combine all ingredients in large bowl (The bigger the better)
  4. Toss ingredients until evenly coated (This could get messy)
  5. Throw on a baking sheet
  6. Bake until desired softness or crunchiness
  7. Enjoy Responsibly! (Careful...they might be hot right away!)
  8. Freeze or refrigerate leftovers for a quick snack (...if there are any left)
